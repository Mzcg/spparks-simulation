app_style thermal_spray command

Syntax: 

app_style style nspins  nparticles lattice_site_size spread_ratio unmelt_fraction spread_ratio_std_dev minimum_final_thickness

style = thermal_spray nspins = number of possible spins nparticles = maximum number of particles to deposit lattice_site_size = size of lattice sites in microns spread_ratio = mean value of spreading ratio distribution between spherical particle diameter and spread splat diameter unmelt_fraction = the fraction of incident particles that are deposited as unmelted hemispheres spread_ratio_std_dev = standard deviation of spreading ratio distribution minimum_final_thickness = the simulation will terminate once all parts of the coating are at least this thick (in lattice sites)

Examples: 

app_style thermal_spray 1  1000 2 2.5 0.01 0.1 40

Description:

Restrictions:

Domain cannot be decomposed into subregions in the z-direction. For parallel simulations, specify decomposition with the processor command.

Only compatible with square cubic lattices.

Related commands:

app_style potts

Default:

As explained above the default for grad_z = 0.0.

(Garcia) A.L. Garcia, V. Tikare and E.A. Holm, "Three-Dimensional Simulation of Grain Growth of in a Thermal Gradient with Non-Uniform Grain Boundary Mobility", Scripta Met 59[6] 661- 664 (2008).
